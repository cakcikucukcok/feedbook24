The events described in the book My Visions from Almighty God are not taken from the authors imagination but are taken from her real-life experiences. This book describes how God spoke with the author in her visions and directed her. The author wants to share Gods Words with every reader of her book in order to serve and please God and to help every reader to succeed in his or her life. The aim of this book is to show every reader the glory and power of Almighty God and to show how God changed the authors life using her dreams, the obstacles of her life, even the cancers that she had. It is a true story about God, his love to people, and how God saved a young Muslim woman and changed her entire life.

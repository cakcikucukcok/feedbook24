From marriage to divorce, and from adultery to the Word of God, author and pastor Ovit G. Pursley Sr. has compiled a comprehensive list of scripture messages to address a wide range of issues that affect todays Christians. Geared toward pastors, ministers, deacons, and all church members, Counseling with Gods Word provides Bible-based guidance. The first section addresses family-related issues, such as marriage, husbands, wives, parents, children and child training, youth, and divorce. The second section covers everything from A to W, including alcohol and drug use, anger, charity, faith, guilt, laziness, poverty, prayer, shame, sickness, tithing, and wisdom. The messages delivered in Counseling with Gods Word serve to save, teach, strengthen, and establish believers in the faith and are a guide for learning to live a Christian existence through the words of the Bible.

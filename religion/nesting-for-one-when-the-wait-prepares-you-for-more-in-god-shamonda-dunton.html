The Bible tells us that before the foundations of the earth, the Lord knew us. He knew everything about us, even before we were formed in our mothers' wombs. And he has even counted every hair on our heads! Yet many of us forget that we have his divine finger upon our spirits, and sometimes when things go God's way instead of our own way, we can feel impatient or abandoned. In this book, author Shamonda Dunton shares the real-life journey of her and her husband's experience with doubt, struggles, and disappointments in the midst of their desires to have a child and start a family. Yet this devoted couple was able to face the challenge of faith and find strength while waiting on the promises of God to manifest themselves, even when years of waiting didn't turn out as expected. But they learned from the experience and now share with fellow believers that everything is according to God's time and will-it just takes patience and the imagination to recognize and accept God's blessings when they come.

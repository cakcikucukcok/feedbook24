This book is an amalgamation and skillful blending of revelation, spiritual insight, academics, research and refined biblical theology. This work caters for the unbeliever, born again Charismatic, Pentecostal, Evangelical, mainline Religious Traditionalist, student, skeptic and atheist there is something for everyone in this revered and well researched work.

This is one of the few books of its kind that blends scholastic wisdom, academic research, and historical actualities with fresh bible based revelation. The author does not bid to coax or cajole you to go with his school of thought neither does he bestow a myopic viewpoint, but uses The Word to explain The Word and balances it with authentic historical facts, substantiated theological facts and leaves you in a place where you can choose either to believe history, astuteness, scripture or academics which are all intermarried in this imposing and revered work. Go through this work, not as a denigrator, but as a student, maintain an open mind and you will just be startled how certain mysteries can be solved so simply through divine wisdom that can still be proved by science, history actualities and adroitly balanced biblical theology.

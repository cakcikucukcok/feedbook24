FROM DEMENTIA TO SALVATION is a personal testimony of God’s amazing mercy and grace for the salvation of an old man who was near death with Dementia.

The Lord laid it on Denise’s heart to pray for and believe God for Arthur to receive God’s free gift of salvation, by hearing the Gospel and believing in the Lord Jesus Christ.

While in the natural it would seem a fruitless thing to do, however, with faith in the all-powerful Creator, Denise believed God for His promises. She needed to have faith and obey the Lord as He instructed her according to His purpose.

As you read this testimony, it is Denise’s prayer that you find encouragement and faith in the Lord for your own life.

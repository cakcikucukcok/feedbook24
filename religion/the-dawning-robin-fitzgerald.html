This story is about those moments when outsiders absolutely had to interfere. The Light Forces stopped an invasion in 1996, originating from an underground reptilian outpost in 'Congo'. Our very patient envoy from these Forces often begins a statement with, "This is actually common knowledge off the planet." The object of "The Dawning" is to make it common knowledge here in order to instill some faith in our guardians. When they put a stop to the 'Bubble Wars' over Congo, the enemy retaliated with what we know as, "The Great Forgetting of 96." Understanding this is paramount when reading the headlines of today... "Inhumane acts continue in Nigeria and Ethiopia...", Cobra has an answer to this, "That's because they're not human." The novel continues to reveal what they are, as they track down as many as they can. He explained in 2012, " ...some were already in circulation before we stopped the '96 invasion."

This book is a preview commemorating the pastoral work of a great man of God. The book let you have a front row seat and take you back through historical tribute of the pastor and the church. You will read a spiritual son comment giving him honor, the inspirational sermon excerpts, bible class teachings and exhortation from Elder Baggett, Elder Young, Elder Davis, and Missionary/Evangelist LaFayette. The words on the pages will stand upright symbolic and give a cool water slap leaving you with the question; were you there at the revivals or church services. Every message is biblical truth for the young, the old, the save and the unsaved. The book let those outside to come in, have a seat and see the vision of a man standing on one led because of his arthritic condition. It helps you feel that old time religion, to meet the man with a loving heart that had a photographic mind; and never forgot a face. The book gives you a glimpse of the iconic, long standing man who left a legacy through his memoir: expressing the word of God.

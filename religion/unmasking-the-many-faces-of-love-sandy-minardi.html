When you release the ugliness of your past, it loses its power and

control over you. A deep healing can begin. Admittedly, revisiting

the past can be devastating, but in God’s unfathomable love and

mercy, you don’t have to stay there. We need to show other

women how to move beyond today and look toward tomorrow,

which is full of hope, love, promise and redemption.

In the end, you will be able to be used for a higher purpose,

God’s calling on your life. As you speak about your victories, you

will recognize the Master’s hand upon your journey and proceed

with joy, confidence and assurance of God’s plan.

Dimensions of a New Civilization lays down standards for uplifting Zimbabwe from the current state of economic depression, into becoming a model to other countries worldwide. A decaying tree provides an opportunity for a blossoming sprout. Written from a Christian perspective, the book is a product of inspiration, bringing reliefs to those having witnessed the strings of unworkable solutions--leading to the current economic dereliction. It outlines the effects of self-centredness as causing such problems, instead of altruism as advocated in Christianity, but neglected by most Christians. Andrew Masuku is also the author of 'The Seed of All Evil vs. The Seed of True Love' published by the University of Zimbabwe Publications in year 2008.

Survey the Entire Bible in Just 90 Days!

For many, reading the Bible is overwhelming and hard to understand and can leave you feeling frustrated and often defeated. The 90-Day Bible Study Guidesolves this problem by taking you on a journey through select portions of Scripture and giving you the confidence to tackle any Bible passage in just 90 days!

Beginning with Genesis and ending in Revelation, you’ll spend just under 30 minutes each day using this comprehensive Bible study workbook with Scripture readings and corresponding Bible study questions designed to guide you to a better understanding of the personalities, the history, the conflicts, the miracles and the Truth that is the Christian faith.

It's the perfect Bible study for beginners, adults, teens, homeschool groups, or those seasoned veterans looking for a refresher Bible study course. To help with those more difficult discussion questions, a complete Answer Guide is available for free online.

The 90-Day Bible Study Guide features:

  • Comprehensive Bible study workbook with studies for each week
  • Study questions that are ideal for group discussion
  • Personal reflection and note taking opportunities in the Bible study journal
  • Answer Guide for all questions and Scripture Reference Guide available for free online
  • Available in print or e-book formats

In just 90 days you will be well on your way to knowing what you believe, why you believe it, and how to apply it with The 90-Day Bible Study Guide.

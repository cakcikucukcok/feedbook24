Are you the person God created you to be or the person Satan molded you into?

The Bible tells us that we are made in the image and likeness of God. Unfortunately we have an adversary, the devil, roaming around like a lion seeking whom he may devour. Most of the time he doesn’t literally kill us, but takes us off the path of God by concealing our true identity. Satan attacks when we are young, when our hearts are soft and vulnerable—easily molded.  Whether we grow up in the Church or come to Christ later in life, we don’t realize we’ve been altered because much of Satan’s work is either subtle or completely invisible. Fear not. We have a Deliverer.

“In Christ you have redemption from all of the devil’s work, including the impact it has on your heart, the reactions it produces in you, and even his work that becomes a part of who you are.” That is what God spoke to Denise Buss, author of the new book Original Design: Set Free to be Who God Created. This book is packed with revelations from God on how Satan uses an array of influences such as our family, our culture, and even the Church to wound and scar us—change us from who God intended us to be. But it doesn’t stop there. Through Denise’s journey you’ll see how God’s mighty hand reaches forth to unbury secrets, gently uproot lies, and heal wounds in our hearts, thereby setting us free from Satan’s grip and restoring us back to God’s original design.

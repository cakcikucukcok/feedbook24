Have you ever asked where do Miracles come from? How do they happen? In this book, you will explore some 20 miracles. These true stories will inspire a sense of great expectancy that God is doing something in the atmosphere. You'll find treasures to help you know that Miracles Are In You. Because Jesus lives, Miracles live! Estella makes it exciting and enjoyable to explore and examine Miracles in our lives. I believe you will begin to see all the miracles around you and become more aware of the miracles which are in you as you read this book. I highly commend to you Miracles are in You. Dianne Haneke PhD, Professor Emeritus of Literacy Education, near Austin TX. Estella Carter is a mother, grandmother, aunt, and a friend to many. She enjoys spending time fishing, crabbing and just being around the ocean. She loves doing different kinds of art work and listening to music. She was a Sunday School Superintendent, teacher, and womens group leader. Estella was ordained in 2003 to the ministry. When God summoned her for another assignment, she dropped every- thing and came to Austin TX, where she currently resides and encourages others to know God is alive and well.

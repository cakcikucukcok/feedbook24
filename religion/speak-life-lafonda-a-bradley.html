Speak Life! Confessions for Every Area of Your Life was written to help Christians change the landscape of their lives through the words they speak. The bible tells us that death and life are in the power of the tongue. This book helps the reader to speak life concerning different situations in their lives. Scriptures pertaining to various circumstances that one might face, have been researched, penned, and used to decree Gods Word in our lives because there is power in our words. Words are a spirit. Words will have a positive or negative effect on that which they are intended.

Our world is changing at a bewildering rate. Although the prophet Daniel lived during an age when technology was relatively simple, he foresaw a time on Earth when many shall run to and fro, and knowledge shall be increased. (Daniel 12:4) How unlikely for a man, living 2,500 years ago, to foresee in his own ability a revelation that has been fulfilled within the last 200 years!

For those embracing the words of Dickens, the assumption could be made that these are the best of times, while anyone objecting to this premise might be judged to suffer from neo-millennium phobia. But undeniably, a darker shadow is being cast upon the stage of current events as the curtain rises one last time for the final act of Apocalypse!

Apocalypse, A prophecy in which events of a great cataclysm and the destruction of all evil forces are foretold.

“You have been like a mother to so many.”                                                                        

That’s hardly any consolation to a woman who desperately wants to be a mother.  A real mother. Not a “like a mother”. Motherhood is comes easy for many women. For others, it is only a dream, until it becomes a nightmare.  The loss of a child by unthinkable, unimaginable, and unfair choices and events    My book helps women who want to be free from shame and guilt get peace and love in their lives again. Its unique advantage is that it was based on the author's actual life experience, plus enhanced by the experiences of women in the Bible. You are not alone, my sister!”  Many people will tell you that the aftermath of child loss is emotional and psychological. They may even purposely avoid the deep inner emptiness that a woman feels when her baby dies.  There is, however, no way to avoid the spiritual nature of child loss. This book helps women to face these issues head on in order to emerge with love, peace and hope of the other side of despair.

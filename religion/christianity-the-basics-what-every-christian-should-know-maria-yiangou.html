This book is aimed mainly at charismatic or Pentecostal Christians who have just begun their Christian walk and have little or no understanding of how it works in church, church jargon, requirements and basic Christian beliefs that are all founded upon the word of God.

These beliefs include how one can receive the prayer of salvation, the necessity of water baptism, how one can be baptised in the Holy Spirit as well as the importance of prayer, reading Gods word, righteous and holy living. After every chapter, the author has written a quiz on the chapter, which will encourage the readers to go back to each chapter and look up the answers if they don't remember. Hence, this book can also be used as a self-study tool or a Bible Study Text book. The final page of the book has a list of memorable moments readers would like to write about once they have started their Christian journey. 

It is a must for anyone new to the faith and written by Pastor Maria Yiangou who is the co-founder of a thriving church in London, England and has weekly television programmes that are broadcast globally on Christian television Networks.

God lives! He wants you to get to know Him through His word - the Bible, and His Spirit. He will teach

you and give you peace for each day as you search for Him. God is not dead! One can know and see this

is true by seeing how many lives have been changed and given a new hope – eternal life. The author

sees this as proof that God demonstrates the He is alive in many millions of lives all over the world.

You will be convinced by reading and pursuing this devotional that God has many individuals under His guidance. His plan for each of them is the beginning of a new life when they give Him their permission. He does not force Himself on anyone. In this book you will find choices to make to be able to see God working and changing your life. I encourage you to take the plunge, it is so worth it!

You will see examples of His unconditional love, His power, and His presence – Omnipotent, Omniscient, and

Omnipresent. There is not enough room to list all of God’s attributes, but you will definately find more compiled in

this book! Choose you this day whom ye will serve.

The Word of the Lord abideth forever. Each day hear His Voice and do not harden your heart with

unbelief.

It is the author’s desire for us to be sensitive to everyone we meet. Just like when we see people, we can

picture them as an individuals created in the image of God, who need a Savior. Whatever you do for

others, you are doing it to/for Christ.

John 17:3

And this is eternal life,

that they know You, the only true God,

and Jesus Christ whom You have sent.

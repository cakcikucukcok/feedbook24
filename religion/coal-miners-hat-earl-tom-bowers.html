Darkness permeates our world. How is a Christian to understand the nature of light and darkness? How do we walk in God’s light, stay vibrant in our faith, and recover from failure? This book pays homage to coal miners for their relentless efforts to provide energy for our world and put meat on their family’s tables. The analogy of a coal miners hat is used to understand the nature of light. The reader will reflect on what it means to walk in the light that is provided and become who God intends us to be

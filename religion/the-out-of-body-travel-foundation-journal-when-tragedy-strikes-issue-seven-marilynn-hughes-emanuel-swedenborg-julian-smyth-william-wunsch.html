OUT-OF-BODY TRAVEL MAGAZINE: The seventh issue of the 'The Out-of-Body Travel Foundation Journal' begins with a peek into the meaning of tragedy and some of the timeless words that have been spoken throughout the years in American History when tragedy has struck, words that touch deeply into our hearts today as much as yesterday. In our Question and Answer Section, we answer the only question that's been coming from everyone around the United States and the Middle East, which is 'Why?' And to restate our faith, in Our 'Different Voices' section, we have included an article entitled 'The Gist of Swedenborg,' compiled by Julian K. Smyth and William F. Wunsch which answers the questions that arise at times like these with a profound restatement of our faith through the eyes of a 16th century mystic.

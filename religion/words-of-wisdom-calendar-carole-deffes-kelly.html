This is a 365-day wisdom calendar that can be read every day, every year for all your life. It will motivate you daily to put others before yourself and to think before you act. Every day there are short words of godly wisdom and an abbreviated scripture. It will guide you through your daily life. The first several pages (introduction) touches on God's unending love for all of us. It attempts to explain the Trinity-Father, Son, and the Holy Spirit. It's a mystery and always will be because it's God stuff, and we're not God. It also attempts to explain why Jesus chose to leave his perfect heaven and come to our world to be beaten, mocked, and crucified.

God's love for all of us is breathtaking!

Want to read the Bible, but don’t know where to start? Puzzled about how it all fits together? Explorer’s Guide to the Bible will take you on a journey of discovery... Explorer's Guide to the Bible is for anyone who wants to know more about the Bible but isn't an expert. The book is divided into three main sections to give readers different levels of Bible engagement, ranging from a general overview to a a close-up look at each book. Explorer's Guide to the Bible is a great starter to use for personal exploration, or to use at church groups and Christian Unions. Key features include: - Explanations of key biblical concepts - Diagrams to demonstrate time scales etc. The book also features articles on biblical words and concepts such as exploring what is 'Covenant' or 'Why all the killing in the Bible?'. Using accessible explanations, diagrams and more, readers can explore the Bible in a new and user-friendly way.Explorer’s Guide to the Bible … a big picture overview. "Shows how the pieces fit together, introducing the Bible’s overarching storyline with its focus on the person of Christ. An ideal starter guide to the book that continues to change the world." Jonathan Lamb, Director of Langham Preaching, Langham Partnership International

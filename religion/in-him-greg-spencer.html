Author Greg Spencer was born again at the age of seventeen. God spoke to him several times in the following years about the plan He had for his life. Following the guidance of the Holy Spirit, Spencer has been equipping the saints by teaching them who they are in Jesus Christ. Now he presents In Him, a yearlong weekly devotional to help Christians receive the revelation of Jesus.

Each new week, Spencer offers a personal reflection or message and offers appropriate Scriptures to frame his ideas. In Him communicates that God the Father has provided Christians with an inheritance and eternal life in abundance. Through these weekly devotionals, Spencer shows how to succeed in the pursuit of and knowing of God.

Heartfelt and inspirational, In Him makes it clear that following in the Masters footsteps means spending time with Him, learning of Him, and demonstrating Him to the world.

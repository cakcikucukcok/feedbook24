Visit 52 churches in a year? How crazy is that?

Join church-reform advocate Peter DeHaan, PhD, and his wife as they spend a year visiting a different Christian church every Sunday: Protestant mainline, evangelical, and charismatic. Roman Catholic too.

They visited a church just like yours. Positive.

In 52 Churches, you’ll discover:

  • How to welcome visitors well.
  • Practices in your church that drive people away.
  • Three greeting opportunities—and how to excel at each one.
  • Key tips for improvement at your church.
  • How to change from being a church consumer to outreach oriented
You’ll laugh. You’ll cringe. You’ll cry.

But 52 Churches doesn’t rip at today’s church. It’s not a church-shopping romp or a mean-spirited journalistic exposé. It’s a gift that offers encouragement, hope, and support to the Christian community. And DeHaan shares helpful suggestions too.

Written as narrative, 52 Churches calls us to embrace all the branches of Jesus’s church and expand our practice of worshiping him. DeHaan invites you to join him on this personal, spiritual sojourn of faith-building encounters.

Read 52 Churches for education. Read it for entertainment. And read it for the insight gained only from visiting a boatload of churches.

Ideal for church leaders, members, and outsiders. And if you’re still reading this, it’s perfect for you too.

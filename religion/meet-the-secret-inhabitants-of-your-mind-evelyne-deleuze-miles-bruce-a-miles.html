Presenting the Characters in your Mind Come and meet your true Self!

Authors, Bruce and Evelyne, help you navigate a spiritual journey to meet the dynamics of your true self and become equipped and empowered to pursue your real destiny Meet the Secret Inhabitants of Your Mind, is filled with ground breaking insight into how our mind functions. Using clever pictorial images, symbolic characters of our mind are illustrated giving understanding on how our thoughts and actions are influenced. In order to heal many times we need issues to be revealed. Bruce and Evelyne give great insight into revealing the source of problems in our lives enabling us to walk in freedom through the healing power of Gods love. I recommend this book to all who want to progress in their God-given destiny with a transformed heart and mind. Rev. Patricia Bootsma, Co-Lead Pastor, Catch the Fire Toronto Airport Bruce and Evelynes book does an outstanding job of bringing a clear Biblical understanding of our minds, hearts and spirits and how they relate. Especially relevant for today is their non-threatening presentation of evil influences on the mind and how, with Gods help, we can effectively deal with them. Chester and Betsy Kylstra, Founders, Restoring The Foundations International

From cover to cover Overflowing Earth proclaims a reassuring truth, "Without a gardener, there will never be a garden."

Overflowing Earthis for those who doubt all the scary predictions about the future of planet earthfrightening pandemics; worldwide shortages of food, water, oil, power, and even pure air. This book explains why all the prophets of doom have repeatedly been wrong and shows that, except for war, mankind's lot on the earth has dramatically improved, especially in the last two centuries.

In contrast to a message of gloom, doom, and disbelief in a Creator God, Overflowing Earthproclaims a message of peace and plenty. Overflowing Earth shows how God designed the earth to be a garden of plenty, filled with human beings who obey and worship Him. If man chooses to work in sync with God, the earth can indeed be a place of abundance, an Overflowing Earth.

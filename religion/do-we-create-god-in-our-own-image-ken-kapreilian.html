Newly revised for 2017. The title of this book is a play on words from the following scripture. " So God created man in His own image; in the image of God He created him; male and female He created them." (Genesis 1:27) In this book we will explore the many different ways in which our view of God is influenced and thus created. If a person believes that they are created in the image of God, then they will act, think and behave accordingly. Conversely if a person believes otherwise (Darwinism) then they will also act, think and behave accordingly. Therefore, a person's thinking and behavior is directly related to what they believe about whose image they were created in. To find the answer to the question of; "Do We Create God In Our Our Image?" is the reason I have written this book. I invite you to explore this very fascinating subject with me.

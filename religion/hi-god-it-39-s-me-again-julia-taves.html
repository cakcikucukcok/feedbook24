Kids have questions and need answers. They look to each other for self esteem, friendship, entertainment, and even help for understanding larger concepts. Why not allow children to look to other mature children for help in applying Gods Word?

When kids see other kids affected by Gods Word and making a difference, they understand and are inspired for life change. Relating and connecting to another child opens doors into the heart of a childs issues andmost importantlyinto the heart of God. Hi, God. Its Me Again is a devotional book written from a wide spectrum of kids who are living out the Gospels through applying Gods Word and sharing that with others. These devotions are a valuable tool to inspire children from ages eight to twelve and to allow God to make a difference through them, just as He has done through the lives of similar children.

Got trial? Ever experience the healing balm of the Savior’s atonement while in the “eye of the storm”? Ever wonder how to not only endure trial, but become better for it? The Sweetness of Trial builds perspective and an understanding of “why” trial happens and teaches you how to become better—not bitter when trial strikes.

Not a “read once and forget it book,” within its pages you will experience a change in how you view trial, gaining a clearer understanding of the part trial plays in who you will become. The author takes you by the heart and leads you to experience the sweetness—a spiritual “runner’s high” if you will—while in the midst of trial, and reveals the source from which this sweetness comes.

A motivational, educational, and spiritual “how to” book which will have you laughing out loud one minute and crying the next, as the author invites you into his personal experiences with trial from youth to adulthood, culminating in a solid understanding of the necessity of trial and how to experience the sweetness which can only be tasted after the trial of your faith.

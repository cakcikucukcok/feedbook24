Please be aware that this is not a study of demonology or another book that concentrates on demons. As time goes on, we shall see the areas that the devil is making sure we do not pay close attention to. We shall be looking closely at the tactics of the enemy and also the tactics that God wants us to use in fighting against wicked spirits, principalities of powers, and all other things that fight against righteousness. This book also looks into leadership, money, debt, and relationships. These are areas that the devil is attacking.

Single life today can be tougher than what most woman desire to admit. There are many struggles single women face each day while waiting for ‘the right one’.

Author Tracey Laviolette openly discusses the struggles she and so many other women face being single. She explains how she overcame her battles with letting go of past hurts, being alone, mistakes made and faith.  Laviolette rips off the bandages with a no-holds-barred approach to enlightening and inspiring the reader with wisdom and God’s word.

“The world can paint a picture of what your life should be, but God has already created a masterpiece of what your life WILL be.” T.Laviolette, Single and Waiting on God's Promise

Single and Waiting on God’s Promise is scripture based, and written from the author’s experiences, and those of whom she’s witnessed. She tackles issues most women don’t want to discuss or admit. The struggle of being single is real, but it doesn’t have to be. God is true to His Promises, and He does not lie.

Are you tired of stagnation in life? Tired of wandering through life without productive results? Are you ready to move to the next level? Joseph believes that by living and applying the principles in The 21 Proofs of Faith, you will free yourself from failure and program your mind and heart for positive transformation, victory and success. If you have been struggling with faith and feel like God is far from you or your prayers just bounce off the ceiling, then you have found the right place to help you out of your situation.

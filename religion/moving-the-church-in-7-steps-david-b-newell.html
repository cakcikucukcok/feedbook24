Step forward!

Moving the Church in 7 STEPs is a commentary and a guide for how the Christian Church, and specifically a single congregation of believers, can move forward without fear and with great courage and boldness to impact the world.

The premise is daring—stating that believers must not allow unbelievers to define the narrative of what is or is not proper engagement for the Church. The author stuns the reader with the truth and honesty of the Scriptures, exposing a level of naïveté in the Church that is astounding. Too many have embraced the values of current cultural winds over and above the teachings of God’s Word.

The local church can infuse God’s Kingdom throughout its community—and the world—with seven Strategic, Targeted, Evangelistic Plans that are clearly defined within this motivating book written by a seasoned man of God. Filled with current, modern-day graphs, quotes, and statistics, you will learn in one volume what it would take months of Internet research to discover regarding the current state of societal and cultural battlefields Christians face.

Each chapter concludes with an interactive set of questions to provoke answers as well as critical thinking that will move you forward in your personal journey with Christ. Step out…today!

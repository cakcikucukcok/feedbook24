Natural Appearances, Natural Liberation is based on the six bardos teachings from a series of hidden treasure texts known as the "Profound Dharma of Natural Liberation through the Intention of the Peaceful and Wrathful Ones," discovered by Karma Lingpa in the fourteenth century. The practice of the six bardos, according to the Nyingma school of Tibetan Buddhism, is essentially the meditative practice of living and dying. This book offers a scholarly but accessible explanation of the wisdom embedded in this ancient Buddhist classic. Master Tam is a leading Sino-Tibetan teacher, and author of more than 60 Buddhist books. This is his first book in English.

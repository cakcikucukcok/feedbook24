Get ready to experience a whole new level of freedom!

Our world is riddled with pain. Hurt is all around us. We get hurt, and we hurt others. None of us can escape it. Eventually the battle with guilt, shame, anger, and resentment takes us over. We may try for a while to wade our way through, only to find ourselves burnt out on ineffective self-help tips and strategies.

What if there is a way to rise above it all and live in a place of unfathomable peace and freedom? And what if this way has already been mapped out for you and all you have to do is say, “I’m in”?

In The Great Forgiveness, David D. Ireland will use timeless lessons from one of the greatest Bible stories ever told to help provide answers to two very important questions:

  • What is the great forgiveness?
  • How can you experience it?

As you read and apply the principles in this book, the load you’ve been carrying will be lightened, and the freedom you've been dreaming about will become your reality.

David D. Ireland, PhD, is the founding and lead pastor of Christ Church, an 8,000-member multisite and multiethnic congregation of over sixty nationalities in New Jersey. Diversity consultant to the National Basketball Association, Ireland hosts national and international seminars on diversity, leadership, marriage, and prayer. He is the author of over 20 books including Raising a Child Who Prays and The Skin You Live In. Visit www.DavidIreland.org to learn more.

Its All In Me is a look into the body, mind, and soul of a Man who does not apologize for his being. In a world that focuses on tolerance, the words in this compilation encourage acceptance of all ideas and beliefs. However, this is not accomplished by taking any political or religious stand, but simply by accepting that we are Man, monster, and miracle. All in One.

In this book, No-Man stands before the judgment of the readers proclaiming, You Can Handle The Truth! Every thought in these poems is complete. As the reader takes in each poem, they will question not only the authors sense of reality, but they will question their own. They may even discover questions to many of the answers they consider fact. But the real hope in this book is that they will take the opportunity to declare, I am a person physically, mentally, and spiritually. It is all in me and I wouldnt have it any other way.

Pastor Billy Wilson wants to bring as much truth to light as possible in his lifetime. In his latest book, Wilson relies on inspiration from the King James Bible to share Gods teaching that in the end, the world will be divided into two kinds of people: those with the Almightys spirit and those that belong to the Mystery Babylon.

Wilson, who urges others to keep an open mind and a calm spirit, examines past political events as well as our current political environment and its impact on Christianity while leading others on an introspective journey where no topic is off the table. Throughout wording intended to motivate spiritual seekers everywhere, Wilson offers encouragement to keep the Lord in our hearts and shun the work of Satan while repenting for our sins.

Mystery Babylon Revealed offers a pastors varied reflections on lifeboth past and presentas he inspires others to know, yield to, and be willingly led by the Spirit of God while on their own spiritual journeys to discover the truth.

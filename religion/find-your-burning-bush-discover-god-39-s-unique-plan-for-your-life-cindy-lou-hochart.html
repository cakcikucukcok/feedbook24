Everyone wants a happy, passionate and abundant life. That is a unifying characteristic of humanity, but happiness and passion for life can be elusive. God put each of us here for a specific purpose, but we often struggle to understand what that purpose is. I often thought that I needed my own Burning Bush so that I could, like Moses, get direction and clarity from God on what He wanted from me. "Find Your Burning Bush- Discover God's Unique Plan for Your Life"" is a practical and easy to read guide for those who are stressed out, lost and confused, or just thinking there should be more to life. By reading and implementing the steps outlined in "Find Your Burning Bush", you will gain understanding of your purpose, the passion that comes with living intentionally for that purpose and have peace knowing you are following God's plan for your life.

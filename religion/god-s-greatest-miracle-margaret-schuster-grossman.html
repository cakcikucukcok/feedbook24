Explaining Jesus being physically with us in"that box", the Tabernacle, is a difficult challenge for most parents. Now parents and elementary religious education teachers have a book that makes it easy! Beginning with miracles marvelously illustrated and a short explanation on colorful text pages, this book draws the child--and the reader--into acceptance and deep belief and love of the Holy Eucharist and an understanding of how every Catholic Church's Tabernacle can contain Jesus in the form of Consecrated Bread. Some of the beautiful illustrations include a child or children with which the child can identify and there are blanks for them to fill in their name/s. Watching for the bunny makes the search for God's Greatest Miracle even more intriguing. Bible references are given for each miracle so it is easy for the reader to go for more details. God's Greatest Miracle is perfect for showing God's personal love for each one of us! This book is a great coffee table book for children!

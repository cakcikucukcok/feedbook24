These poems are meant to encourage those

Who now are in despair

Those who knowingly have made the choice

To put their souls in for repair

Giving themselves unto the Lord

And following His instruction

Studying daily in his word

Is helpful (in my deduction)

This book is full of Truth and Grace

And I intended it to be

Help for the entire human race

If they take time to read and see

When you sing praises to God above

And count your blessings so real -

He will send to you, abundant love

Changing the way that your heart feels

In this book there is encouragement and hope

Peace, Joy, and giving of Thanks

Pure sweet love that can help you cope

And fill in most of life's blanks

There is no need for dread or for worry

If you have chosen Jesus Christ as your way

His promise is "to receive you in glory"

Because you have refused to let Satan sway

I have written this book to be uplifting

Most readers, I won't be privileged to know

I pray these verses a blessing

As through this life's journey you go.

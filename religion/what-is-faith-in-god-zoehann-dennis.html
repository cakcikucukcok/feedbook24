Nothing from God comes without faith in Him; you must first believe and be ready to take that leap of faith, that risk and step of uncertainty with full and complete trust in Him in order to receive from Him what youve asked for in prayer. This book will challenge you to have faith in God and do so even more, especially in the most impossible situations of your life! I dare you to believe when everyone else has given up. As you read, you will begin to resist the temptations to doubt God and start experiencing His blessings upon your whole life in magnificent, jaw-dropping ways. Doors that were closed will open for you! Lost opportunities will find you! Your confidence will be resurrected! The favor of God will accompany you! Let the Creator create your miraculous path to great accomplishments and for his glory. Remember that the steps of a righteous man are ordered!

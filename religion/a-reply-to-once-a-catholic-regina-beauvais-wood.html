A Reply to Once a Catholic contains subjects that will help to answer many questions concerning Catholicism. Catholics and Protestants who are seeking to better understand the traditions and teachings of the Catholic Church will benefit from this book. Scripture is included to help clarify the teachings of the Catholic Church and it will be very useful to better understand the Catholic faith. This book will challenge you to search your heart, mind, soul and Holy Bible.

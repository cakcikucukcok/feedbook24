What happens when life deals you a hand and you cant see how you can possibly win with what you have been dealt? How do you cope when things dont go the way you plan or think they should go? How do you respond when your expectations do not come to fruition?

Losses (of any kind) in this life are inevitable and cause us to feel wounded, violated, and left unattended. The purpose of this book is to help facilitate the healing and wholeness that will manifest in our lives after we traverse through the stages of grief.

When life deals us a hand that we would rather not have, we rely on and trust in the truth of Gods word to bring us to victory!

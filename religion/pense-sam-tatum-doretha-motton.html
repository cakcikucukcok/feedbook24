Philosophy, science & theology have been long divided. Pense is written to show evidence that all scholarships are born of the same root, which is Universal Truth, and the Bible is the foundation of universal truth. Within scripture are evidences, symbolic references and other material that go overlooked because of the division of the opinions of men. Pense attempts to clearly make these unifying distinctions so to give life to intellectual endeavors that go without purpose, and are today mere facts and loose information. But, if man began to see all learning as a single unit, its singular guiding root as God, then the debates and struggles for superiority will quickly fade.

Movies, TV shows and fairy tales often paint the picture that “love conquers all.”

 

The reality is as time passes, we find out that “love” really doesn’t conquer all. We discover that making love relationships last requires persistent work. It also requires commitment and sacrifice from both people in the relationship to keep the flame of love burning brightly. When that doesn’t happen the relationship can become unhealthy or plain unbearable.

 

Surviving a troubled relationship is one of the most difficult challenges many individuals will ever face. Once the difficulties arise there is a critical decision that has to be made, which is should we break up or make up?  This book provides strategies to navigate the aftermath of the relationships’ falling apart. as well as those couples who are still hanging on thinly by a thread in a severely challenging or troubled relationship. Unfortunately, most of us know people in these situations, and if you are one of the many individuals who has been “riding out” a see-saw/rollercoaster relationship battle and trying to decide whether to hang on or quit, this book was created just for you.

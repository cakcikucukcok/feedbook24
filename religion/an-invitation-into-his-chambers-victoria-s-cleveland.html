"An Invitation into His Chambers...The Power of Intimacy with God" will help you discover the secret to a deeper connection with God through a revelatory romance! You will learn how deeper intimacy will produce power in prayer. This book will teach you how to experience Him with all of your senses. Through a series of dreams and visions, Vickie illustrates truths that allow you to have greater intimacy with God. With new tools at your disposal, you'll learn how to pray with authority and make more authentic declarations of faith. Through this deeper connection with God, you'll discover who you were meant to be and how best to walk in the FULLNESS & POWER that He intended. God wants a relationship with you. This book will help that relationship flourish.

Individual chapters include teaching and truths on effective communication, creating an atmosphere, using all your senses to deepen intimacy, and foundational obedience. Also included are truths on God's desires, passion that produces prayer, corporate intimacy, the resting place, and challenges ahead.

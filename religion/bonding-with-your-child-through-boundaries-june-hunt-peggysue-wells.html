Children need boundaries in order to flourish. In Bonding with Your Child through Boundaries, biblical counselor June Hunt offers parents biblical principles, time-tested wisdom, and practical advice for setting up appropriate boundaries in their homes. First, Hunt highlights the essential role of boundaries, explaining why they’re vital and how they positively impact a family’s life. Hunt then outlines 36 practical “how-to” steps for making boundaries work. Featuring real-world examples and guidance for responding to specific challenges, this book will help parents cultivate happy homes and healthy relationships with their children.

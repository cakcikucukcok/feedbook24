The Elijah Moment is for the intercessory prayer warriors daily meditation to give you peace in the midst of the storm. The Elijah Moment is your moment with God in devotion. The Elijah Moment truly takes a moment in your life to return to God for specific requirements for daily living. The devotion is written as if you are studying the Word and praying with me. It only takes a moment. That moment is life-changing! Its a life-changing moment with God, prayer, coffee, other warriors, and myself. You are encouraged to invite others with you during the walk, and include me in your walk as well.

Love Letters To My Queen Bride are God's words to His Bride to heal, comfort, encourage, and edify her. The four sections are titled Love for My Beloved, Healing for My Beloved, Comfort for My Beloved, and Direction for My Beloved. Our prayer is that by reading this book you will fully receive His love, comfort, healing, and direction.

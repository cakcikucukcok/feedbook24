Life is full of challenges and there are negative aspects of life that every human being must experience and endure.  Struggling and suffering are two of these aspects.  People struggle to make ends meet, to find their purpose in life, and even to understand the existence of God.  Suffering can be inflicted upon us or the result of decisions we made.  Do we struggle or experience suffering because we have done something wrong? Is God punishing us? Why me? Nobody Wants to Struggle, Nobody Wants to Suffer will help readers answer these questions and more. This book will:

  • Encourage those who are discouraged or hopeless about their situations.
  • Provide a new perspective on 21st century struggles and sufferings.
  • Demonstrate how to handle adversities and how to help others overcome adversities.

Bad things can happen to us on any given day, however, it's our perception of these events that will determine how we handle them. Know that God is our source and our resource and He has ordained a victorious life for us.

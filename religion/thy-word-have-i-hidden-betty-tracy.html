This is a book of memory verses and a chatecism. Written by a homeschool mom for use with her own children. In fact, we use this book every school day. The Tracy's (Dad Andy, Mom Betty, Joy, Jim, Jonathon, Joseph, Jessica, Jennifer, Jacklynn, Jane, and Joshua, plus Jiffy dog, Stella cat, a Gecko, Beta fish, four finches, six rabbits and 22 chickens) are active in their church, pastored by Betty's dad, and enjoy long drives in the nearby farmlands and sunsets from their front porch.

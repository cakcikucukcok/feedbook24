About the Book

PLEASE PRAY! THIS IS URGENT! HELP!

Have you heard these words? If someone asks you to pray for them, do you know what to say or what to do? Did you know that you could pray for others, using the Word of God, and see results?

Now that I have your attention, I want you to glance inside of this book. What do you see? The Word of God within a prayer! In this book, you will read prayers of over 130 different requests that came to our Internet group of Prayer Warriors in the past two years. Each request includes Our Scripture and Our Prayer of agreement. Matthew 18:19-20 (KJV) if two of you shall agree on earth as touching any thing that they shall ask, it shall be done for them of my Father which is in heaven. [20] For where two or three are gathered together in my name, there am I in the midst of them.

Over the past two years, the Prayer Warriors have prayed for hundreds of people and received updates regarding countless miraculous healings. Praying the Word of God has brought healing to some and yes, a few received their healing when they went to heaven. Even though our hearts were grieved, we knew we had done all we could do and that was to pray. James 5:16 (KJV) pray one for another, that ye may be healed.

I trust this book will help you with you prayer life. Keep it handy, who knows, one day you may be called upon to be the one who will be Praying with Authority!

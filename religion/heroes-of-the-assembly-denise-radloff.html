HEROES OF THE ASSEMBLY is a collection of prophetic daily devotionals which focus on spiritual warfare.

Ephesians 6:12 tells us that we do not wrestle or battle with flesh and blood but against principalities and powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, and against spiritual wickedness in high places.

God has given us ways to overcome the powers of darkness and everything which rises up against us as believers.

In these devotionals Denise Radloff shares her own prayer messages with you. As you read them and the associated Scripture verses you can listen to what God is laying on your heart and be encouraged and uplifted by His promises and encouragement to you.

May these devotionals inspire you to seek greater intimacy with God.

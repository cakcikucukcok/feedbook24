Your church workers are your winning team; you win or lose base on how you choose, manage, train or treat them. There is nothing worst in Church work than untrained, poor, ignorant church workers, because they will misrepresent what you stand for. As a pastor your success in ministry depends on your workers. You can't have Church growth or any meaningful progress without the intelligences, faithfulness, dedication, competences and financial contribution of your Church workers. No one succeeds alone; where there are no train workers your vision will perish, show me a church worker and I can tell you who the pastor is!

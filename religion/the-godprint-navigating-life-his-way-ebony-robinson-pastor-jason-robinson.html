In a world filled with trials, tribulations, temptations, and fears it can be a bit difficult to stay on track with God's plans for our lives. How do you stay focused enough to veer away from possible destruction? How do you remain optimistic when the burdens of this life seem too heavy to bear? Join Minister Ebony Robinson as she offers a refreshing and eye-opening approach to establishing and developing a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. You'll learn how to effectively meditate, study, and pray in order to understand and unleash the God-given power that resides in you. By faith, you will discover and operate in your God-given purpose, so that you may live the abundant life that God has called you to live.

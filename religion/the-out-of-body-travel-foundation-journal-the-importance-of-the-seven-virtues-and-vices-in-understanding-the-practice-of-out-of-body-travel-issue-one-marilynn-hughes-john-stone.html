OUT-OF-BODY TRAVEL MAGAZINE: The Official Magazine of the Out-of-Body Travel Foundation containing articles on Out-of-Body Experiences, as well as, an author question and answer section and articles from other sources on Out-of-Body Travel and other areas of spiritual interest. The first issue of the 'The Out-of-Body Travel Foundation Journal' discusses the importance of the Seven Virtues and Vices in understanding the practice of Out-of-Body Travel, as well as, answering some questions about who can have these types of experiences and whether they can be verifiable by exterior physical means! Excerpts from the 'Catechism of the Catholic Church' and 'The Christian Virtues: A Book on Moral Theology for College Students,' By Charles Sheedy are highlighted. Our special 'Different Voices' section in the issue contains an article on Lucid Dreaming, by author John Stone.

The world you will abandon keep in faith only to those who practice chaos and tyranny. But

you, you will remember them, who deceive and mislead. You will become wise and

strong, able to distinguish those who live in fear of others and those who live with courage

and fortitude. Wisdom shall become your stronghold and you shall discern all things.

Light shall shine throughout your days. You will remember faith cannot be placed in the

dead on behalf of the living. This book cannot be written with kind words. It will not

be poetic. It exposes the desensitization process; deterring its followers into oblivion. It

demonstrates the disregards for social and moral rule. It renders shame and guilt, good

and evil. Prevailing false hope; contained within is an opposition to an attempt to bring

repressed thoughts or feelings into consciousness.

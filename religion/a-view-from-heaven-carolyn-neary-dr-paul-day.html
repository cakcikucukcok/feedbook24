This book is a unique combination of written pictures, artwork, music and devotional reflections that draw us to a view beyond the earthy veil.

God desires to talk to us and show us views from heaven even more than we long to see them. This book invites the reader to boldly grab hold of the heavenly promise that when we seek for Him with all our heart, we will find Him.

Finding the strength to go through one tragedy at a time is difficult. Finding the strength to endure one catastrophic change in your life after another is nearly impossible without the presence of God in your life.

This novel takes you into one womans journey through such an experience. Jenny, the main character, is faced with many life-changing events all within a few years time. A chemical exposure while working as a surgical technologist in a suburban hospital takes away her health and career. Meanwhile her father is lost to cancer and her mother is diagnosed with cancer two months later. As Jenny is put on disability and faces her own mortality, her husband of twenty-seven years leaves her for another woman. She begins to question her belief in God, blaming Him for her situation and her health. All of this while she is in the midst of a multi-million dollar lawsuit!

Regaining her faith in her Savior was the first step in putting her life back together. Jenny emerges from her trials a stronger, more spirit-filled, woman as she proves that all things work together for the good, to those that love the Lord. Romans 8:28

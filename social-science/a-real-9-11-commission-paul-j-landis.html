"A Real 9/11 Commission" was created to make available a growing body of Evidence by the end of 2004, that what the Bush administration and the Mainstream media was providing was not an accurate account of the events of that day. With the publication of the 'no blame' report by the Kean Hamilton 9/11 Commission there were national poll results that indicated for over 40% of the respondents, Kean and Hamilton did not consider facts that would contradict the official 9/11 story. "A Real 9/11 Commission" considers all facts and evidence available, whether or not Kean and Hamilton considered them. "A Real 9/11 Commission" was created also to provide the opportunity for those interested, on-line and/or off-line, to 'Render A Decision" based upon their review of the evidence, as if they were members of this 9/11 Commission

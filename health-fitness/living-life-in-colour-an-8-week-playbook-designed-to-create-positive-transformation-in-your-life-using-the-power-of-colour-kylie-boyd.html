Are you ready for a makeover?  Well, this is a makeover with a twist, designed to create a positive transformation on the inside and out. By consciously choosing what colours you wear and surround yourself with, you will expand your feelings of peace, motivation, joy, self-confidence, creativity and most importantly, attain a greater sense of fulfilment.  

If you’ve ever yearned to be that someone going on an amazing quest to live a better life, then wish no more. Living Life in Colour is a fun eight-week Playbook that lights up your world in different weekly colours and promotes the benefits of these with simple daily activities.

This week-by-week approach will have you:

• Making small changes with BIG impact

• Using colour to positively affect yourself and others

• Looking at the world through different eyes

• Clearing out the old and bringing in the new and fresh

• Living your life with a renewed sense of purpose

Living Life in Colour is about making the journey of your life more balanced, more fun, more creative, more fulfilled and more colourful, until you’re living the life you’ve always dreamed. So if you’re ready for an adventure, this Playbook is ready for you.

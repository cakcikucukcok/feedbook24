This book is an inspiration and will motivate you to look at the challenges in your life in a new way! The authors who have shared their stories expose their own struggles with emotional chaos that were triggered by situations or circumstances, where they lost a part of themselves along the way. They share their journey to overcome overwhelm and life altering challenges to move forward in their lives. From becoming a single mother to being diagnosed with a life threatening condition, from abuse to fear, there is an obstacle that will touch each one of you. It is possible for you to overcome and become the adventurer you always wanted to be. Enter the lives of others, and find the inspiration to transform your life today! Our True Short Stories were written by these wonderful co-authors.

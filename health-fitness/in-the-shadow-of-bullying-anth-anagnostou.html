“Do you believe that everyone has the right to live and be accepted for their individuality? How do you think a victim of bullying feels? If you are a victim, you will find solidarity! If you have witnessed such incidents, you will find the way and the courage to tackle bullying! If you bully others, you will understand the pain that you inflict on people! Life is full of surprises. One moment is sufficient for you to lose all those things that gave you happiness, allowing sadness to take over. But, it is up to you to get out of the shadow and into the light!”

Anthi Anagnostou is a Sociologist, born and raised in Kastoria (Greece). In the last few years, she has moved to Novara (Italy) where she lives with her family. This is her first novel.

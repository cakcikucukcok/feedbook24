In Freedom Unleashed, Dr. Niaz Ahmad Khan F.R.C.S., Ph.D., makes the case that while a world without taxes may seem like a pipe dream, its within our power to create. He examines how a mutual benefit bond system would allow nations to abolish taxes, fund initiatives, create incentives for rich and poor citizens, promote sensible public and private investments, and eliminate poverty. The systems incentive-based principals would also help governments rebuild national infrastructures and restore law and national pride. While Malaysia is used as an example, the principles he shares could help countries throughout the globe eliminate taxes, create wealth, and improve the quality of life for its citizens. People may laugh at the idea of a tax-free world, but they also laughed at the notion of sending astronauts to the moon, splitting the atom, and curing diseases. Open your mind to a world of possibilities with Freedom Unleashed: How to Make Malaysia a Tax Free Country.

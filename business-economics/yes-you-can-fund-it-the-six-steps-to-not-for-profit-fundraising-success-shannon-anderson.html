Do you need to generate income across multiple revenue streams for your non profit organisation? Is your existing fundraising strategy not getting the results needed? Are you sick and tired of the churn of fundraising activities or people through your organisation? Yes You can Fund It is written for CEO?s and Development/ Fundraising Managers to build or re-build their organisational fundraising strategy from the ground up. Stop wasting time and precious resources on the latest fundraising and marketing ideas. Start building the right foundations and make informed choices about the fundraising tactics that will work best for your organisation. Yes You Can Fund It  takes you through a simple process to:

•Establish the six essential building blocks for fundraising success

•Understand your fundraising income profile

•Understand how you can make changes to this profile over time

•Choose the most suitable fundraising tactics for your organisation

•Develop a workable fundraising strategy and resource plan linked directly to your organisational strategy. Shannon Anderson, an award winning fundraiser, has generated more than $30m for not for profits over the past ten years.

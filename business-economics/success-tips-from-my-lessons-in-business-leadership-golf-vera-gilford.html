One out of three businesses fail in the first year. Fifty percent fail in the first three years. Maximize your chances for business success with these tips to drive profits up. Learn to identify and strategically manage or eliminate toxic disruptions that threaten the success of large and small businesses. Identify patterns of sabotage that occur in all businesses. Discover new markets, including 80 million Generation "Y" or Millennials that could be your customers or 10,000 baby boomers turning 65 each day. Learn the importance of rhythm and alignment when merging with companies across the globe. Connect with the soul of your business. This book helps you to discover your X-Zone where you master your inner game and where passion and purpose merge to produce excellence in products and services and rewards you with financial and meaningful gains.  Learn success principles of golf that can be applied in business as in golf.

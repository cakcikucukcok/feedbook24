Aren’t you tired of living paycheck to paycheck? Tired of deciding which bills to pay when in order to save yourself an overdraft? You aren’t alone, not by a long shot.

Financial freedom sounds like a pipe dream to most of the world’s current population. Not because it’s as unattainable as it’s made to sound, but because of how much more saturated the markets are and how much less equipped we are to break into them as a whole. Many people think that in order to make money you have to focus all of your time on it and be able to fully devote yourself to doing so. Isn’t this what we are taught in school and from the media?

The truth of the matter is that that just isn’t the case. You don’t need to come up with an entirely new business idea and start it from the ground up in order to manage yourself financially. You don’t need to spend a ton of money on ‘deposits’ or books with step by step programs. Everything you need is at the click of your fingertips, just waiting for you to discover.

Am I going to have to buy a step-by-step program? No! Absolutely not.

This book is going to teach you how to make and manage your own money in a way that continuously builds, not ask you to buy into some get rich quick scheme that ends up only driving you further into debt in the long run.

We’re going to cover how to start up a passive income, how to get ahead monetarily, saving, investing, building your portfolio, and most importantly, how to keep your progress continuous! Anyone can save a hundred dollars and keep it in the bank, but we’re talking about continuously adding to those numbers.  This book isn’t about fast money, it’s about sustainable money- and the easily incorporated practices in which you can finally master your own money instead of allowing it to master you.

You don’t need to spend money to make money- you need to save it, and save your bank account in the process.

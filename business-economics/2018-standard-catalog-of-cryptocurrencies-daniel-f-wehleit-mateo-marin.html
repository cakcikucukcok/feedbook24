Since the emergence of Bitcoin and cryptocurrencies, the selection of coins has grown from a small handful, to hundreds, all attempting to establish their home within this revolutionary paradigm. The labyrinth of alternative cryptocurrencies has seen the rise and fall of countless coins. For enthusiasts, critics and potential users trying to make sense of it all, it’s hard to determine which coins deserve our attention and why. Understanding what each currency is trying to achieve and their difference is imperative to seeing why blockchain will reinvent the way we live. This book is a catalog of one hundred select cryptocurrencies, explaining the concepts behind these coins and detailing their unique facts, histories, directions and ultimately their place in the crypto-maze.

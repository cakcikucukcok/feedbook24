How to Build Trust includes over 1001 Conversation Starters that anyone can use to build better relationships at work. The questions are clear and in plain english. They are specifically designed to build a connection between the person asking the question and the person answering.

The questions are accompanied by quotes and light hearted commentary so that the book is both fun and engaging.

 

This book is suitable for leaders who want to: network; build a connection with co-workers; get the best out of others; and facilitate team building sessions.

Topics cover everything from: important people; music, memories to values

EXAMPLE EXTRACT: Habits

I have been trying to write a book my entire life but I couldn’t.  Spoiler alert – I finally wrote this book. What changed? I got into the habit of writing more often.

It turns out that habit is the secret ingredient to nearly everything in life (except chocolate cake – the secret ingredient to chocolate cake is LOTS of chocolate).

So much of what we do it out of habit. Asking someone about their habits can be a simple way to understand a person better.

 

  • What is your strangest habit?
  • What is your best habit at work?
  • What is your most expensive habit?
  • If you could break any habit, what would it be?
  • What habit is the secret to your success?

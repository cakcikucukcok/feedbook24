Complete summary of Stephen M. Covey's book "THE SPEED OF TRUST: The One Thing That Changes Everything".

The review of the ideas in Stephen M. Covey's book " The Speed of Trust" shows that trust is not something which is merely "nice-to-have" but is instead a hard-nosed business asset which can deliver quantifiable economic value. Therefore, it makes good financial sense to consistently find ways to enhance trust levels both within and external to your business organization. This summary highlights that you need a workable mental model to build and enhance trust and it also describes five contexts in which trust is established.

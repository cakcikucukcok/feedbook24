We have a serious problem with zombies, and we are all infected. 

There is a communication virus that creeps through every home, every office, and every auditorium. 

We live in a world where our colleagues are overwhelmed by an ocean of communication noise, our loved ones are lost in a blizzard of social media, and our audiences are addicted to their mobile devices. They've stopped thinking, and they gave up listening some time ago.

The world of communication is changing, becoming less human, and it will not end well.

We are responsible for turning this around. We can be the difference the world secretly craves. We can be the cure the world is yet to realise is indispensable. We can, and we must, defeat the rise of the Modern Zombie horde.

Join me and explore the human strategies, techniques, and adventures that will enable you to rise up, wield your metaphorical machete, become a communication zombie slayer, and achieve incredible success at work, at home, and beyond.

 

Nick Looby is a communication specialist, a survivor, a human, dedicated to curing the modern zombie virus with decades of horde battling experience. He needs your help. We all do. Time to take a stand. The communication battle has begun.

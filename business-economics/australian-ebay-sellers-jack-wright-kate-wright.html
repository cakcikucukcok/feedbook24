There are many books out there that can help you sell on eBay. But they are not for everyone, nor are they easy to understand.

Do you sell the odd item here and there on eBay? Want to sell a whole lot more and bring in the cash?

This book has been written in plain and simple talk by two Aussies that make money on eBay. Good money. And we have fun while doing it. 

Learn how we started out with a small eBay income and increased it tenfold in a matter of months.

We discuss every step of our eBay process, how we do it, and why we do it. We can help you make a few dollars, or a few thousand. It's up to you, and we will explain how. 

The book is full of handy tips, do's and don'ts when it comes to finding stock, listing stock, and selling stock to make you money. 

Inside you will find sections on:

- Finding items to sell

- Tips for successful listings

- Tips when pricing

- Dealing with eBay

- Dealing with difficult eBay customers

- Do's and Don'ts in every step of the process

And much more.

All discussed in easy to understand non-technical language, and with a little Aussie slang and humour thrown in for the ride.

There's no better way to learn than have a laugh while you're doing it.

Corporate Closets holds a vast array of information for the reader who is looking for practical and intelligent ways of taking his/her organization into the future. This book discusses the challenges at two levels: one while operating in this current environment of fast and constant change, increasing customer demands and disruptive competition; another while planning, implementing, and managing transformation toward high performance and growth to overcome them. Embracing the disciplines of learning organizations, this book explores the much-needed shifts in mind-sets, behaviors, and skills. Topics highlighted included corporate innovation, corporate agility, customer focus, team learning, and knowledge in worker engagement and collaboration. With a better understanding of the underlying complexities, disabilities, and difficulties, the author has developed a step-by-step guide to formulate action strategies to future-proof any organization, whether big or small. Francis KC Lim has taken two decades worth of experience in the corporate world and condensed it into an educational text to help others achieve sustainable change and performance for future success and survival.

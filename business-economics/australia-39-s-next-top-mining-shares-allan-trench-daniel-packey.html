Australia's Next Top Mining Shares is a companion book to the widely-acclaimed A Sharebuyer's Guide to Investing the Australian Mining Boom. In this new book Trench presents the next best resource stocks through the lens of the commodities mined in Australia today. Each commodity is profiled in detail and then companies exploring, mining and even those in feasibility phase in these commodities are profiled. So investors can follow, say, Gold, and then refer to Trench's expert opinion on Gold in the short-term and review the gold-mining and producing companies listed in the ASX for investment opportunities. Over half of the 1,800 stocks listed in the ASX are in the mining and resources sector. Serious investors will be clambering to access the information in this new book.

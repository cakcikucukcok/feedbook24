My first experience of an agency was back in the mid 1990s. Since then I have set up and built various successful businesses in the property, marketing, recruitment, publishing and digital sectors. I can still relate with all of the issues and processes that take place on a day-to-day basis, as if feels like it was only yesterday that I worked in estate agency myself.

In this book, we aim to form a general idea about the Just Order and bring out the truth and try to demonstrate that the Islamic, scientific and humanly projects don’t consist of aerial, imaginary discourses and slogans. Thus, we are glad to give a chance to the people to make a contribution with their positive criticism and proposals besides resolving several askance (false illusions and understanding) and answering the questions, related with the Just Order Project.

 

    We hope that you will find sufficient and cohesive answers of these questions in this book;

 

     1) From which needs does the Just Order arise?

     2) On which values and sources is it based?

     3) In the Just Order:

  • Economy and Trade
  • Government and Politics
  • Law and Justice
  • Education and Merits
  • Morals and Religion
  • And how will be the dignity in the foreign politics?

     4) How will a new civilization that is based on the peace and abundance of peace throughout the world be established?

     5) How will our territory and humanity be saved from the wheel of tyranny and exploitation?

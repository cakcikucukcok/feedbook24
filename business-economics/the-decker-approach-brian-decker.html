This book, "The Decker Approach - A Common Sense Approach to Retirement Planning " is a detailed description of how our company, Decker Retirement Planning Inc., helps people in retirement or within 5 years of retiring, try to receive the income they need and want for the rest of their lives.  Our clients can see how much they could draw each month which helps give them priceless peace of mind.  We help our clients try to minimize their taxes.  All of their taxes.  We try to help our clients minimize the risks they face in retirement, which includes stock market risk, interest rate risk, and credit risk.  We try to help our clients fully protect the assets that they have taken a lifetime to accumulate from liability risk and long-term care risk.  Finally, we try to make sure that our clients have the liquidity that they need for life's emergencies.  At Decker Retirement Planning Inc., you will be dealing with a fiduciary, who is required by state law to put our clients best interest before our companies' best interest.

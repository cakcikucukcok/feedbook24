"A snowflake is the most beautiful form of water"

That is one of the observations of a lifelong skier who:

  • Discovered a skiing paradise in the Canadian Rockies
  • Experienced a few hard lessons in a snow school

  • Skied with Olympic champion Nancy Greene
  • Tried to ski the same track as World Cup racers
  • Let his mind win over matter when he broke his ribs skiing
  • Became a professional ski instructor at the age when most people retire from their jobs
  • Spent a season teaching at the local ski hill
  • Tells about it all in this entertaining book, a blend of travel, sport and memoir, written with gusto, humour, and love of skiing.

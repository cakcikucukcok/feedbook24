This is not a sad tale, although it has terrifying, potentially soul-destroying, elements. Rather it is the story of a man of integrity, strength, and ingenuity who has overcome many of the scourges of our modern world – abuse and addiction – while living a productive and creative life. Dan tells his story in his compelling and natural voice, weaving back and forth from his childhood in Saint John, New Brunswick to Toronto and finally to Belleville, Ontario.Along the way, you will fall in love with Young Danny and his evocative telling of the world and times he lived through. 

You will cry at how his Devil stalks and abuses him. You will cheer and laugh at how Dan uses his wits and humor to make his way through his life and your heart will melt at the love Dan finds with his Angel. 

Dan’s story will ultimately lift your spirits and remind you that there is light at the end of a dark tunnel.

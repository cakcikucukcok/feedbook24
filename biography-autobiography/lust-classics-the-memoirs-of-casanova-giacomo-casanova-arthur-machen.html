"I leave to others the decision as to the good or evil tendencies of my character..." These highly amusing memoirs by notorious womanizer Giacomo Casanova are quite possibly the greatest true stories every written. Casanova takes us down the road of his long and adventurous life from getting arrested for affront to common decency to escaping prison, wooing royalty, scamming left and right, all the while bedding a different woman every night. He traveled all over Europe, encountered one historic person after the next, among these Madame de Pompadour and Voltaire, making his accounts the most authentic source of the European social customs of the Eighteenth century. Plainly said: this is the sexiest and most entertaining history book you will ever encounter. This is a collection of the 6 volumes about Casanova.

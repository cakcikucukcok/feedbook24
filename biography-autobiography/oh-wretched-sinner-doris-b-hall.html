In February 2011, God blessed me by allowing me to publish my first book. It is an autobiography of my life about the challenges of growing from a child to a woman. The title of this book is OH Wretched Sinner by published author Doris B. Hall. OH Wretched Sinner tells the story of how God has allowed me to share my sins in hope to stop people from making the same mistakes that I have made in the past. By using my life as an example and my book as a resource you can find a better life. You do not have to wander around the mountain for forty years searching for answers that I can provide here in my book. This is a self-published book by Xlibris Publishing Company.

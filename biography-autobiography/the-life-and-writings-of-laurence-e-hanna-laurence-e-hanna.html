Laurence (Larry) Hanna, author, grew up on a farm in Oklahoma during the Great Depression and the Dust Storms of that era. He has compiled short stories of incidents in his life as well as in others during that time. He served his country during World War II, spent over 40 years in the oil, gas and pipeline business. In his recent life, a retirement center seemed appropriate where he could josh with other old people about the "good old days". At age 94 he decided to make available for reading some of those earlier experiences all the way from the one room school to an office in New York City. Life changed dramatically from the "Outhouse" in the 1920s to the exploration of Mars in the 2000s. . Stories about making love on the front porch to life in a penal institution. Stories that will grab your imagination.

A children's book recommended for children ages 6 to 9.

The purpose of this book is to assert sleep as a repair element.

Through the adventures of Martín, children will learn about the importance of sleeping to have a better performance at the school, and to live a better life.

I don't want to sleep! is a book for early childhood education, suitable to be read aloud to children. It is also suggested for children that are starting to read.

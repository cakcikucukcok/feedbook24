SHADES OF THE WOLF (The Pack)

By Karen Whiddon

After Anabel Lee's husband is killed, she begins seeing and hearing ghosts. Like all the others, Tyler Roger has a special request and needs Anabel's help. Killed in a fire, Tyler has come back to save his baby sister, Dena, from a deranged warlock and serial killer. No matter how hard he tries, Tyler cannot locate his sister… He needs a human to help save Dena's life. Racing against time and magic, Anabel and Tyler search for his sister…but if they happen to find love, will it be doomed before it even begins?

Allison Siegel is out to stop a wedding, or at least stage an intervention to convince one of her best childhood friends not to marry the woman she's sure is all wrong for him. The only problem is they have a mutual friend who doesn't want her to butt in.

Clint's loved Allie since she grew out of pigtails, but she never saw him as more than a friend. Even though she doesn't know it yet, he's being a really good friend by stopping her from getting all "My Best-Friend's Wedding". And he's ready and able to convince her to let the sparks now between them build into a fire.

Allie can't believe how good Clint looks all grown up, and she certainly can't believe he's holding her captive in the backwoods of Montana until he thinks she's come to her senses, or their friend's wedding goes off without a hitch.. But Allie isn't going to let a little thing like a six-foot-something sexy forest ranger stand in her way...

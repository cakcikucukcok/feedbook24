Natalie Thorton, Dean of the Sydney Conservatorium of Music, is driven and professional and doesn't have room in her schedule for relationships. She also knows everyone who's anyone in Australia's music industry, which is why Jaxon Campbell is sure she can help him and his band find a perfect new lead singer. Trouble is, the last time he saw her, he kind of broke her heart and stole her cherished, autographed AC/DC album.

Natalie doesn't want to feel anything when Jaxon strides into her office after all these years, but she does. And if he's going to ask something from her, well, she wants something from him. Orgasms. Lots of them.

How can Jax say no to that? He's never forgotten Natalie, and it's not like they're going to fall in love. But just who's playing who? And whose heart is going to fall first?

Each book in the Heart of Fame series is a standalone story that can be enjoyed out of order.

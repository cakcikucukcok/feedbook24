Teddi stumbles accidentally on a corpse in the deep freeze at

supermarket snob (among other things) of a mother will never

let her hear the end of it.

Nor will Drew, who has told Teddi time and again she's got

to stop messing with murder scenes. Until Teddi goes from

material witness to potential next victim

But the woman whose smarmy ex dubbed her "Long Island's

Most Dangerous Decorator" isn't going down without a fight.

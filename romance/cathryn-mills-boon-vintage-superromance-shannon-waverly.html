Cathryn McGrath has a reputation to uphold in Harmony, Massachusetts–as the perfect wife, homemaker, mother and friend. Until her husband has an affair with another woman, and walks out on Cathryn and their kids…

He becomes involved, deeply involved, with her and her kids. And he shows her that there's life after betrayal, love after divorce.

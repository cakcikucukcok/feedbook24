The love story of Wynn Cary and Doey Brooks, based on a true story, is as compelling today as it was when it began. Set against the violent backdrop of World War II, Wynn and Doeys journey explores the hopes, fears, passion and call to duty that have altered the lives of young lovers since the beginning of time. Yet theirs is a uniquely American story. Wynn Cary has dreamed of becoming a pilot since his youth. At the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, he falls in love with Doey, a beautiful young Duke University law student who shares his passion for flying. As their relationship grows, the war stalls their plans to marry. But their poignant story is living proof that love never dies.

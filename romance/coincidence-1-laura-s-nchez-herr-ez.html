Charles Green is a Scotland Yard detective with a reputation among the force as being once of the best. He's tough, relentless, with nerves of steel and with the highest rate of solved cases. His professional life as well as his personal life seem to be solid rocks that keep him steady during the hard times that every police officer endures during their career. However, Adriana Soto is a girl that's beginning as a police officer whose entire life is upside down. Her professional life just started, her personal life is absolute chaos and on top of that, she has trouble following orders if she doesn't think they make sense. And that, in a profession such as hers, will cause her many problems. More so having coincided with Charles Green on the first case during her internship. But it's obvious that a certain type of people translates danger into a unfettered passion that may end up consuming them completely...

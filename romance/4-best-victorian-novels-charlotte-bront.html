Charlotte Bronte wrote „Shirley” in difficult days for her: at this time her brother Branwell and two sisters, Emily and Ann, died of tuberculosis. Jane receives an excellent education and tries to achieve everything in life herself. Having installed herself as a housekeeper in the family of the rich and mysterious Mr. Rochester, Jane not only finds her „I”, but also experiences the strongest feeling possible. The multibook includes the most read novels of the author, such as: „Jane Eyre”, „Shirley”, „Villette”, „The Professor”.

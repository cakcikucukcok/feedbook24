The first book of my book The Witch and The Preacher Act One is about Toni Black and Ted Smith.  Toni is a Christian Witch who loves the Lord with all her heart, body and mind.  She meets her new neighbor who happens to be a preacher and his name is Ted Smith.  They basically seduce each other and fall in bed.  They have a wild weekend and then Ted takes her to church and confesses his love for her and she freaks out and runs away from the car when they get back to their apartment building.  Ted can’t keep up with her so he loses her and she locks herself in her apartment.  He catches up with her and beats on her door.  She doesn’t answer and he gives up and goes to his apartment.  What follows after that is one hell of a wild ride.  She runs from him and gets a cottage on her employer’s land.  He spends money looking for her.  She stays hidden and don’t let him know where she is at.  Days goes by and she come to terms with his confessing of love.  She goes to the court house and teaches him a lesson he will never forget.  Then they both go to his church and teach the congregation a lesson that they won’t forget.  They get together and live with each other.  Ted gets really sick and she sticks by his side through thick and thin.  This is a powerful love story about unconditional love and accepting each other as is.

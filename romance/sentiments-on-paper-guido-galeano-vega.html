This is a small book of poems, inspired by real people and authentic feelings.

Its purpose when created was to magnify the sublime feeling of love that awakens in men a beautiful woman.

A virtue that men must not lose is the ability to love, comprehend, tolerate, and protect women. For obvious reasons, they are the guides of our existence, which may be good or bad, but is ultimately a unique experience that is priceless and offers us the privilege of existing and living. We owe this to love and to women.

Thus, the existence of this humble book of poems.

They've loved each other all their lives 

At eighteen, Claire and Dean were planning their future--until they suddenly found themselves parents. With Dean on the road pursuing a football career, raising their daughters became Claire's first priority and full-time job. 

Now, with the girls grown, she can finally pursue her long-deferred dream of going to college. Then she gets the news: she's going to be a mother again. Coming to terms with her midlife pregnancy means confronting her hopes, fears and the choices she's made. Through twenty-five years of joy, struggle and near-tragedy, the one constant was Claire's love for Dean. Is that love strong enough to weather the biggest crisis of their marriage and help them embrace the futuretogether?

And having an element of irreducible rascality in her nature, Josy takes to the challenge.

Her backyard neighbor, FBI Special Agent Nicolas Hawk, watches as Jo turns her backyard into a boot camp exercise course with a jogging path, trampoline, a wire from the roof, and ropes to climb up the house. When he sees her three-ring-circus routine, hes hooked and has to know, What the hell is going on?

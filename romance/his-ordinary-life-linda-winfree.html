When it seems like Del Calvert's teenage son is somehow involved in a murder case, he's thrown back together with his ex, Barbara. He's ready to do whatever it takes to save his son and keep his family safe--and they are still a family. Being around Barbara again makes him realize how much he didn't appreciate what he had when he had it.

Barbara Calvert is afraid to need anyone--especially the man who still holds her heart, whether he knows it or not. But with her son in a complicated situation that's put their whole family in danger, she has no choice but to lean on Del and work together. Del wants a second chance, and while she doesn't know if her heart can take being broken again, how can she not take a risk on the man she still loves?

Each book in the Hearts of the South series is a standalone story that can be enjoyed in any order.

Naomi was a timid computer nerd until she created 'Snog', a social networking app that's taking the internet by storm. Suddenly she's running an international company and it's all a bit overwhelming. But her friend Jeni resents Naomi's success and can't forgive her for stealing away her ex-boyfriend Simon. Jeni wants Simon back and she knows just how to go about it, by creating a fake profile and luring Naomi into an online flirtation with a fictitious man. As Naomi's relationship with Simon begins to wobble, Jeni offers a shoulder to cry on and plenty of sympathetic advice, although her online mischief making is becoming even more extreme and it's only a matter of time before she gets found out. Can Simon and Naomi forgive each other or has too much damage been done?

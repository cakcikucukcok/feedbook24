Beautiful Jessica Tyler was born to a perpetual drunk who committed suicide-but not before years of bringing drunken, abusive, men in and out of their lives. Years of having to witness the effects on her mother embedded into Jessica's resolve to NEVER need a man... Rich, charming, Edward Broshworth, Jr. owns the Phlashs Hockey Club, as well as many other assets. This has made Edward a very wanted, eligible bachelor. Between his looks and his riches, Edward can get anything and anyone that he desires. Now he has his sights set on Jessica Tyler. Jacob Brentwood, Captain of the famed Philadelphia Plashes, stands at 6'3" and weighs 240 pounds. He is everything on and off the ice. He is handsome, rich, can melt any woman with his smile, and denounce any man with his scowl. At the height of his hockey career, he can have any woman he wants, but needs no one....until now. Two men who hate each other fall in love with the same woman-but only one makes it out alive...

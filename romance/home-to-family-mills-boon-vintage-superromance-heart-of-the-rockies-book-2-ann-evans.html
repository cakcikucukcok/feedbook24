To Leslie Meadows, the warm, close-knit D'Angelo family is everything a family should be. Everything her own family wasn't.

Then Matt uncovers a secret about her that his father has kept for decades. Once again, he's there to help her deal with the truth. Only, this time they're becoming more than best friends. And the rest of the D'Angelos are looking forward to making her an official member of the family.

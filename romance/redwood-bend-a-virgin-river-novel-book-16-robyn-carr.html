Virgin River- Book 16

One moment can change everything

Former actor Dylan Childress left the L.A. scene behind years ago for a quiet life running an aviation company in Montana. But with business slowing down, Dylan is starting to wonder whether he should take one of the offers Hollywood keeps sending his way. Travelling to a weekend away with friends these troubles are left at the side of the road when he spots a woman stranded with twin boys.

Katie Malone’s trip to Virgin River is stopped short by a flat tire. To make matters worse, it’s raining, the boys are hungry and Katie is having trouble putting on the spare. So when some bikers pull up beside them, offering to help, all Katie feels is relief. Until she sees Dylan. The attraction between the two is instant. But Katie’s a sensible single mother and Dylan’s a die-hard commitment-phobe. Neither one is looking for long-term romance. But sometimes it takes only a moment to know you’ve found something that could change your life forever.
